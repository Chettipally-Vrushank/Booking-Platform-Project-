About Our Project

Welcome to the Train Ticket Reservation System! Our project aims to provide an easy, efficient, and user-friendly platform for booking train tickets. With real-time updates and seat availability checks, you can now reserve your train tickets in just a few clicks. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we've got you covered.

Features include:

  • Login and Register for personalized booking experience
  • Easy seat reservation and ticket booking
  • Real-time train schedule updates
  • Secure payment system
